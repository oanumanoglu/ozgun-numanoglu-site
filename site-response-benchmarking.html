Site Response Benchmarking: Practical Considerations

Jan 22, 2025 · 3 min read

Notes on comparing site response analyses against field evidence and instrumentation data. The aim is to keep a pragmatic workflow while retaining technical rigor.

  • Align soil profiles and motions with the most recent geotechnical logs and regional records.
  • Record processing choices (filtering, baseline correction) and test sensitivity to them.
  • Capture uncertainties explicitly; communicate envelopes rather than single traces when appropriate.
