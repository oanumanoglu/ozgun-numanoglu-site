Perspective

I approach geotechnical and earthquake engineering with an emphasis on:

  • Applying advanced numerical modeling to better understand the seismic performance of infrastructure and energy facilities.
  • Integrating tools such as LS-DYNA, PLAXIS, and DEEPSOIL (now RSSeismic) into practical project workflows in a fast and efficient manner while preserving the technical depth.
  • Linking field observations with analytical models to develop evidence-based conclusions.
  • Engaging in collaborative problem-solving from early project stages through post-event investigations.

while keeping the fundamentals of soil mechanics and observational methods a top priority
